## Built on Hermes Agent

Doby is a community-maintained skin/persona layer on top of **Hermes Agent**
by [Nous Research](https://nousresearch.com), released under the MIT License.

- Upstream: <https://github.com/NousResearch/hermes-agent>
- All AI/agent functionality, tool calling, memory, skills system, gateway
  integrations, etc. come from Hermes. Doby contributes:
  - A persona file (`SOUL.md`)
  - A skin (`skins/doby.yaml`)
  - Two small skills (`forget`, `relogin`)
  - Packaging, install scripts, and one small upstream-bug patch applied at
    build time (`display.compact` config-shadowing fix)

If you find Doby useful, the credit for the heavy lifting belongs to the
Nous Research team and the Hermes contributors.
